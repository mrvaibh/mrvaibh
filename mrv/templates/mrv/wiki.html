Vaibhav Shukla (pronounced: [ʋaː◌̃bʱəʋ ʃʊklaː ]), better known as Mr.VaiBH, is an Indian Software Developer and Engineer from Dwarka, New Delhi, India. He is known for his programming and problem solving nature via his corporation MRV. In July 2020, he scored 1st rank in HackerRank daily challenge competition. In March 2021, his open-source organization got verified on world's leading Internet hosting and version control service GitHub.

Early life[edit]

Vaibhav Shukla was born on 11 August 2003 in Basti, a districts of Uttar Pradesh state, India, and a part of Basti Division, where he is based.[2] He attended school till 2021, when he completed education he pursued his Software and Web development career;[2] skipping his Bachelor of Technology graduation because of his nervousness about passing the exam. He later completed his schooling through long-distance learning.

Vaibhav's younger brother, Anubhav, wants to become an Actor and Dancer who, with the stage name Mr. EX, has collaborated with him on his many singles.

Career[edit]

As Developer[edit]

Popularly referred to as MrVaiBH, or just 'vaibh', Vaibhav is known for his very powerful Coding skills. He is mainly involved in creating projects/programs that helps human existence to live a better life. Apart from this, he also contributes in some Open-Source Projects. Vaibhav has NO ONE in his team as he manages to do all the work on his own.
